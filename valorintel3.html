VCT Game Changer's has started

Published on November 28th, 2023

VCT Game Changers, VALORANT's all women esports league has officially kicked off it's biggest tournament of the year. This year Game Changers is located in Sao Paulo, Brazil. The tournament will be lasting from November 28th through December 3rd, where the Grand Finals will be livestreamed on Twitch. This year's Game Changers is stacked with talent, here is a list of the teams competiting in this years Game Changers:

• Evil Geniuses (North America) • Shopify Rebellion (North America) • BBL Queens (EMEA) • G2 Gozen (EMEA) • Team Liquid (Brazil) • KRU Blaze (Brazil) • Team SMG (APAC) • Edward Gaming (East Asia)
